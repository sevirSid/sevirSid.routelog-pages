RouteLog Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Introduction

RouteLog ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our route tracking application handles your information.

Information We Collect

RouteLog collects the following information solely for the purpose of providing route tracking functionality:

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates to track and record your routes
  • Route Information: Distance, duration, and timestamps of your tracked routes

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by RouteLog is stored locally on your device only. We use this information exclusively to:

  • Display your current location on the map
  • Record and save your routes
  • Show your route history and statistics
  • Provide route replay and analysis features

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All your location data and route information is stored exclusively on your device
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not upload, sync, or store your data on any external servers
  • No Data Transmission: Your location data is never transmitted to us or any third parties
  • Your Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app

Third-Party Services

RouteLog does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties.

Location Permissions

RouteLog requires location permissions to function properly:

  • "When In Use": To track your route while the app is active
  • "Always" (Background): To continue tracking routes when the app is in the background

You can modify these permissions at any time in your device settings. Denying location access will prevent the app from tracking routes.

Data Retention

Your route data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. You can delete individual routes or all data through the app's settings.

Children's Privacy

RouteLog does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general use and does not target children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Delete individual routes or all data
  • Revoke location permissions through device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data
